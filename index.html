A Little Bit About Me

Hi, my name is Curtis. I am currently a Production Supervisor at a PCB (Printed Circuit Board) Manufacturing facility. I manage the Surface Finish Department with 8 different processes and 24 employees. I am responsible for tracking priority orders through my department, approving/declining vacation requests, scheduling overtime, working with engineering to make changes to processes, tracking quality by reject codes, annual performance reviews, and much more.


I love everything electronics related and have a passion for PC's. I have built 3 of my own personal computers, a home theater PC (HTPC), a personal Unraid Server with a Plex Media Server docker and a docker for doing automatic file backups, and sadly haha, :) my prize possession, a high-end gaming rig complete with a custom watercooled loop.


I am learning how to code professionally and look forward to using those skills in a new career path in Full Stack Web Development. I hope you enjoy my page and project portfolio. Please feel free to contact me via any of the ways listed on my Contacts page. I look forward to hearing from you!
